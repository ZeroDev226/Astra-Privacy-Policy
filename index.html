Privacy Policy

At Astra, we take your privacy as seriously as your productivity. This policy explains how we handle your data and our commitment to security.

1. Introduction

Astra ("we," "our," or "us") provides a productivity platform designed to help you manage tasks, habits, and focus sessions. This Privacy Policy describes how your information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application and services.

2. Data We Collect

To provide a seamless synchronization and authentication experience, we collect the following information:

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication.
  • User IDs: Unique identifiers used to manage your account and synchronize data across devices.
  • App Activity: We store your tasks, habits, focus sessions, and progress data to provide the core functionality of the app.

3. How We Use Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Astra experience:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and secure your account.
  • Synchronization: To ensure your tasks and habits are consistent across all your devices.
  • Progress Tracking: To calculate your streaks, levels, and productivity statistics.
  • Nag Mode: To deliver persistent notifications as configured by you.

4. Data Storage & Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • Local Encryption: Your data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database using SQLCipher (AES-256 encryption) on your device.
  • Encryption in Transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using Secure Socket Layer (SSL/TLS) technology.
  • Cloud Storage: We use Supabase, a secure backend-as-a-service, to store and sync your data in the cloud.

5. Third-Party Services

We partner with the following services to provide our app:

  • Supabase: Used for authentication and cloud database synchronization.
  • RevenueCat: Used to manage in-app subscriptions and premium features.

Each of these services maintains its own privacy policy regarding how they handle data.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You have full control over your information:

  • In-App Deletion: You can wipe all your local and cloud data permanently through the "Clear User Data" option in the App Settings.
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time, which will remove all personal information from our servers.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
